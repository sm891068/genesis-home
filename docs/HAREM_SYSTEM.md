# Harem System Documentation

## Introduction
This document outlines the comprehensive specifications for the Harem System in the Genesis Home project. The aim is to provide clarity on the various components of the system including character requirements, intimacy levels, story mechanics, rewards, interactions, and UI design.

---

## SSR+ Character Requirement
1. **Definition**: SSR+ characters are the highest tier of characters in the game, featuring unique abilities and stories.
2. **Selection Criteria**:
   - Characters must have a minimum base stat of 120.
   - Must be obtained through special events or ultra-rare drops.
   - Characters can be combined or upgraded through gameplay mechanics.

## 11-Level Intimacy System
1. **Overview**: Intimacy levels determine the depth of relationships players can build with characters.
2. **Levels**:
   - Intimacy ranges from Level 1 (Stranger) to Level 11 (Soulmates).
3. **Unlock Requirements**:
   - Each level requires a specific amount of interactions, gifts, and achievements to progress.
   - Special events or side quests may enhance intimacy gain.

## Story Unlocking Mechanics
1. **Mechanics Overview**: Certain storylines and interactions are unlocked based on intimacy levels and character arcs.
2. **Requirements**:
   - Characters must reach Level 5 intimacy to unlock their individual story arcs.
   - Additional side quests might be necessary for further story progression.

## Rarity-Specific Rewards
1. **Overview**: Different tiers of characters offer varying rewards based on their rarity.
2. **Rewards Structure**:
   - **SR characters**: Standard rewards including in-game currency.
   - **SSR characters**: Exclusive items, character enhancements, and lore pieces.
   - **SSR+ characters**: Unique skills or abilities and access to special events.

## Interaction System
1. **Overview**: Players can interact with characters to increase intimacy and unlock stories.
2. **Types of Interaction**:
   - **Gifts**: Players can give gifts to characters, each affecting intimacy differently. 
   - **Quests**: Completing quests with a character can boost intimacy.
   - **Conversations**: Engaging in conversations through dialogue trees.

## UI Design Specifications
1. **User Interface Overview**: The UI must be intuitive, showcasing characters and their intimacy levels clearly.
2. **Design Elements**:
   - Character Profiles: Clearly display intimacy levels and unlock criteria.
   - Interaction Buttons: Easy access to 'Gift', 'Quest', and 'Talk' buttons.
   - Progress Bars: Indicate intimacy level progression visually.

---

## Conclusion
This documentation serves as a guideline for the development and implementation of the Harem System within the Genesis Home project. Consistency and clarity in adhering to these specifications will enhance gameplay and player engagement.
