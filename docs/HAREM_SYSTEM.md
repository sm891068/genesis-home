# Harem System Specifications

## Character Rarity Requirements
- **SSR+**: Special characters that can be unlocked through events or specific in-game achievements.

## Intimacy Levels
1. **Level 1**: Initial bonding, opening basic interactions.
2. **Level 2**: Deeper conversations and minor quests.
3. **Level 3**: Unlocking personal stories and special gifts.
4. **Level 4**: Major quests and defining moments in the storyline.
5. **Level 5**: Final personal arc resolution and ultimate rewards.

## Story Unlocking
- Completing certain quests related to characters unlocks exclusive storylines.
- Key milestones in intimacy levels lead to pivotal story moments.

## Rewards System
- Players receive rewards for reaching intimacy levels with characters, including: 
  - Exclusive items
  - Special dialogues
  - Event participation opportunities
  - In-game currency bonuses

## Conclusion
The harem system enriches the gameplay experience, offering players varied paths based on their interactions with characters, enhancing both story and gameplay.
